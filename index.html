Arun Advani

I am an Assistant Professor at the University of Warwick, Knowledge Exchange Fellow and Research Associate at CAGE, and CRETA Fellow. I obtained my PhD in Economics from University College London, with Richard Blundell and Imran Rasul as my advisors.

I am also a Research Fellow at the Institute for Fiscal Studies. From 2011 - 2013 I worked there on issues of energy and environmental policy. My work there now (2014-) is focused on issues of tax compliance, as part of TARC.
