Annual Wealth Tax (1% above £10m) 1% tax on the value of individual wealth in excess of £10m. Wealth includes net value of all assets, so includes pensions, property (accounting for share owned), and subtracts debts (including mortgages). Tax applies only to the part of wealth in excess of £10m, so someone with £11m in assets would pay £10k in tax. 11.1bn A wealth tax for the UK,

1. Figures in the table are based originally on the Evidence papers linked. They have been uprated by the growth in GDP (or forecast GDP for "five years time").
